Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about our services

What payment methods do you accept?
We accept all major credit cards (Visa, MasterCard, American Express), debit cards, and digital wallets through our secure Stripe payment system. All payments are processed securely and encrypted.
How long does the consultation process take?
Initial consultations typically take 1 hour. For Standard and Premium packages, the complete process can take 2-4 weeks depending on the complexity of your case and the visa type you're applying for.
Can I upgrade my package after purchase?
Yes, you can upgrade your package within 7 days of your initial purchase. Simply contact our support team and we'll help you upgrade with a prorated payment for the difference.
What if my visa application is rejected?
We provide a money-back guarantee for Standard and Premium packages if your application is rejected due to our error. We'll also provide guidance on appeal options and next steps.
Do you offer refunds?
We offer a 30-day money-back guarantee for all packages. If you're not satisfied with our service within 30 days, we'll provide a full refund. After 30 days, refunds are handled on a case-by-case basis.
